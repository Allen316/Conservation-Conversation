Conservation Conversation

Welcome to our conservation chatbot! This chatbot is designed to educate the general public about endangered species and conservation efforts related to those species. Some animals you can learn about are tigers, orangutans, and more! To begin, click the chat icon at the bottom right corner of your screen and you will see a small screen pop up. Then, prompt the chatbot about the animal you want to know about, and it will share some fun facts and conservation information! Enjoy!

Questions to ask:

- Give me facts on ______

- Tell me about ______

- Tell me more

- Tell me about conservation
